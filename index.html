Đề Kiểm Tra Tổng Hợp (40 Câu)

Từ vựng (Giao thông, Phim ảnh, Lễ hội) & Ngữ pháp (Liên từ, Cấu trúc So...that)

Circle the letter A, B, C or D to indicate the correct answer to each of the following questions.

1. You must always wear a ________ when you ride a motorbike to protect your head.

2. A person who is driving a car on the road is called a ________.

3. Cyclists must always keep both hands on the ________.

4. The film last night was so ________ that we fell asleep.

5. Most families have a big feast with roast ________ on Thanksgiving Day.

6. We saw beautiful ________ at a parade at the Tulip Festival.

7. ________ the heavy rain, the La Tomatina festival was not cancelled.

8. ________ it rained heavily, we still went to the cinema to see the new comedy.

9. The plot of the film was fascinating. ________, the acting was quite poor.

10. He is a very careful cyclist, ________ he had a small accident yesterday.

11. The pedestrians stopped safely ________ the traffic lights turned red.

12. ________ the bad weather, our flight to Spain for the festival was delayed.

13. The reviews about the film were excellent, ________ I decided to buy a ticket.

14. The motorist didn't fasten his seat belt. ________, he got a heavy fine from the police.

15. The comedy was ________ funny that all the audiences laughed until they cried.

16. The traffic was ________ heavy that we couldn't arrive at the parade on time.

17. Everybody wore beautiful ________ at Halloween night, so it was hard to recognize them.

18. ________ having a map, the tourists still got lost in the city.

19. The Cannes Film Festival is famous for its Golden ________ symbol.

20. The documentary was ________ moving that many people cried.

21. ________ the terrible weather, we had to cancel the picnic.

22. ________ it was raining heavily, he went out without an umbrella.

23. The test was very difficult; ________, I couldn't finish it on time.

24. I was very tired, ________ I went to bed early.

25. The coffee was ________ hot that I couldn't drink it.

26. She passed the exam ________ she studied very hard.

27. ________ his injured leg, he still won the race.

28. He is very rich. ________, he is not happy.

29. The book was ________ interesting that I read it in one day.

30. We stayed at home ________ the storm.

31. ________ working late, she still woke up early the next morning.

32. They played well, ________ they lost the match.

33. He ran ________ fast that nobody could catch him.

34. She didn't study at all. ________, she failed the test.

35. ________ the traffic was heavy, I arrived late.

36. ________ he is old, he runs five kilometers every day.

37. I forgot my keys, ________ I couldn't get into the house.

38. They enjoyed the trip ________ the bad weather.

39. The music is ________ loud that I can't concentrate.

40. He loves playing football; ________, his parents want him to focus on his studies.

Những câu bị bôi đỏ là câu trả lời sai, đáp án đúng đã được hiển thị bằng màu tím nhạt.
